Asana Design System

At Asana, we want to design empowering experiences for our users. We solve challenging interactions at the outset, and then apply the solutions consistently across Asana — making sure people only have to learn them once.

What is the ADS?

The ADS is a collection of components defined by shared principles and patterns. The ADS allows for rapid iteration using a shared vocabulary across design, engineering, and other disciplines.
