The color palette is a cornerstone of Asana, giving the product an immediate sense of being familiar and warm. The color palette leverages Asana’s brand heritage, and builds on its sense of energy, intelligence and confidence. It adds warmth and a human touch.

Asana is committed to complying with AA standard contrast ratios for all text that is not “incidental”. The color combinations in this guide don’t comply with the AA standard just yet. Make sure you don’t rely on color alone to convey state or meaning.
